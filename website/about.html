About Us

Helping consumers make informed decisions during festival shopping seasons.

Our Mission

Empowering Smart Shopping Decisions

We believe that consumers should have access to transparent pricing information to make the best purchasing decisions, especially during high-stakes festival seasons.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, PriceWar Analytics was born out of a simple observation: festival season sales are becoming increasingly complex, with dynamic pricing, flash sales, and platform-specific discounts making it challenging for consumers to identify genuine deals.

Our team of data scientists and e-commerce enthusiasts came together to create a solution that would cut through the noise and provide clear, actionable insights into pricing trends across India's leading e-commerce platforms.

What We Do

We collect, analyze, and visualize pricing data from Amazon and Flipkart to help you understand:

  • Price Comparison: Compare prices between Amazon and Flipkart across different product categories with our comprehensive search and filtering tools
  • Pricing Trends: Analyze price trends over time and identify seasonal patterns to determine the optimal time to make your purchase
  • Market Insights: Explore detailed market share and category performance analysis with comprehensive insights into pricing strategies
  • Interactive Dashboard: Access real-time statistics, charts, and quick access to all analysis tools in one comprehensive dashboard
  • Data Visualization: Interactive charts and graphs including price by category, trend analysis, and comparative visualizations
