Key Observations

Seasonal Patterns

  • Q1 (Jan-Mar): Prices remain stable post-holiday season, with minor fluctuations.
  • Q2 (Apr-Jun): Gradual price increases leading up to summer sales.
  • Q3 (Jul-Sep): Steep price drops during monsoon sales.
  • Q4 (Oct-Dec): Most volatile period with major sales events.

Conclusion

The price trend analysis reveals strategic pricing patterns that can inform both consumer purchasing decisions and competitive strategies.

  • Optimal purchase timing aligns with major sales events
  • Platform-specific strengths in different product categories
  • Price matching is common between platforms
